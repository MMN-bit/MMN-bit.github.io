Help & Language

What To Know

  • Rubiks cube notations: notations are signified with a letter representing the face to be turned (e.g., F for front, R for right). If there is an apostrophe after the letter, it indicates a counter-clockwise turn (e.g., F' means turn the front face counter-clockwise). If there is a number 2 after the letter, it indicates a 180-degree turn (e.g., F2 means turn the front face twice).
  • Use the button 'Start Shuffle' to start a shuffling animation.
  • Press 'Stop Shuffle' to stop the animation before applying a solving method.
  • You can paste a custom scramble in the input field and press 'Execute'.
  • 'Beginner' is easiest to follow; 'CFOP' and 'Roux' are more complex methods.
  • Rotate the cube with your left click mouse button to inspect all faces.
  • Move the cube in space with your right click mouse button.
